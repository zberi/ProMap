Standard Operating Procedure: Accounts Payable (AP) Payment ProcessingObjective: To ensure accurate, authorized, and timely vendor payments while maintaining strict internal controls and segregation of duties (SoD) to prevent fraud and errors.1. Roles & Segregation of Duties (SoD)To maintain proper internal controls, no single individual should have the ability to initiate, approve, and execute a transaction.AP Clerk: Responsible for invoice data entry, 3-way matching, and initiating the payment proposal. No system access to approve payments or modify vendor bank details.AP Supervisor / Finance Manager: Responsible for reviewing the payment proposal against supporting documents and granting first-level approval.CFO / Designated Director: Responsible for final payment release/sign-off (second-level approval). No system access to data entry.Procurement / MDM Team: Responsible for vendor onboarding and updating bank details. Completely segregated from the AP processing team.2. Step-by-Step WorkflowStep 1: Invoice Verification & 3-Way MatchResponsibility: AP ClerkAction: Match the vendor invoice against the Purchase Order (PO) and Goods Receipt Note (GRN).Control: Any price or quantity variance exceeding $\pm5\%$ must be routed back to Procurement for resolution before proceeding.Step 2: Payment Batch GenerationResponsibility: AP ClerkAction: Extract due invoices from the ERP and generate a weekly Payment Batch (Check/ACH/Wire).Control: Ensure no duplicate invoices are included and early-payment discounts are utilized where applicable.Step 3: First-Level Review & ApprovalResponsibility: AP Supervisor / Finance ManagerAction: Verify the payment batch against the digital trail (Invoice + PO + GRN).Control: Confirm vendor bank details in the batch match the approved Master Data File. Approve the batch to move it to the banking portal.Step 4: Final Authorization & ReleaseResponsibility: CFO / Designated DirectorAction: Review the finalized batch in the banking portal and execute the secondary digital signature/approval.Control: Dual authorization is strictly required for any total batch or single payment exceeding $\$10,000$.Step 5: Reconciliation & ArchivingResponsibility: AP Clerk / GL AccountantAction: Match bank debit confirmation with the ERP ledger within 24 hours of payment release. File the paid invoice voucher marked as "PAID" to prevent duplicate processing.
